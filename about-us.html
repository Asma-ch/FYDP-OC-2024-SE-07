Our History

In 1969, Stylora, a small spinning mill, was established. Over the next half-century, the business grew and gained worldwide recognition for producing premium-quality fabric, crafted with dedication and excellence.

Despite the challenges faced by the textile industry, Stylora remained steadfast in its pursuit of innovation and continued to thrive. In 2014, Stylora the brand was born after fifty years of experience in the industry and a steadfast devotion to quality.

The launch of Stylora marked a significant milestone for the company and a new chapter in the brand's journey. The company's focus shifted to producing clothing that was both stylish and functional while maintaining its premium-quality standards.

Today, Stylora is a globally recognized brand known for its commitment to quality and its passion for creating exceptional clothing. Despite its success, the company remains true to its roots and continues to prioritize excellence in all aspects of its operations.

Brands

Since its inception in 2014, STYLORA has undergone an extraordinary evolution and growth trajectory. The company has successfully diversified its product range, expanded its store network, and established a global online store that proudly serves customers in over 25 countries.

Its mission is to redefine fashion with continuous innovation and deliver inspirational experiences in a responsible way, tailored to the modern customer's lifestyle. At the core of STYLORA's operations, the company is deeply committed to providing superior quality,trend-leading designs, and delivering superior customer service.

Our Sustainibility

Our history is rooted in a deep sense of responsibility towards each other, the environment, and local communities. As a business, we embrace this value and are confident in our ability to make a positive impact on society.

We are committed to reducing plastic usage in our stores, creating sustainable collections from recycled materials, powering our stores with solar energy, and advocating for fair pay and equal opportunities. We believe that corporate social responsibility is not just a buzzword, but a critical component of our success.

We believe that by working together, we can create a brighter and more sustainable future. We are confident in our ability to inspire others to join us in making a meaningful difference and to lead by example in every aspect of our business.
