HOW TO GET THE RIGHT FIT

To measure your clothing size, follow these instructions:

KAMEEZ (Inch)
  • 1. BUST: Measure around the fullest part of your bust. Keep the tape not too loose or tight.
  • 2. WAIST: Measure around the slimmest part of your waist. Usually above the belly button.
  • 3. HIP: Measure around the widest part of your hip. Keep the tape parallel to the floor.
  • 4. KAMEEZ LENGTH: Measure length from the shoulder point to your desired point.
  • 5. SHOULDER: Measure from one shoulder point to another across your back.
  • 6. SLEEVE LENGTH: Starting from the shoulder bone, measure down to the desired length.
  • 7. ARMHOLE ROUND: From the highest point on your shoulder, measure around the armhole.
  • 8. WRIST ROUND: Measure around your wrist. Keep the tape not too loose or tight.
SALWAR (Inch)
  • A. WAIST ROUND: Measure around the part where you normally tie your Salwar (Lower).
  • B. SALWAR LENGTH: Measure from where you tie your Salwar down to the desired length.
  • C. THIGH ROUND: Measure around the fullest part of your thigh. Keep the tape parallel to the floor.
  • D. KNEE ROUND: Measure around the knee. Keep the tape parallel to the floor.
  • E. ANKLE ROUND: Measure around the ankle of your foot. Keep the tape parallel to the floor.
CHOOSE THE RIGHT SIZE

That’s easier said than done. Because, what do you do if you are in between two sizes? Well, that’s up to you. Do you like a tight fit? Go for the smaller size. Loving a loose fit? Go for the larger size.

Top Tip: If your body measurements for bust and waist result in two different suggested sizes, order the size from your bust measurement.
